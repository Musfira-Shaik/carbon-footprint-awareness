# Carbon Footprint Awareness Website 🌍

A comprehensive web application designed to help individuals understand and reduce their carbon footprint. This project promotes environmental awareness and provides actionable steps toward a more sustainable lifestyle.

## 🎯 Features

### Home Page
- Overview of carbon footprint impact
- Educational information about different emission sources
- Tips and strategies for reducing environmental impact
- Call-to-action to calculate your carbon footprint

### Carbon Calculator
- **Interactive Calculator** that estimates annual CO2 emissions based on:
  - Transportation (car miles and flights)
  - Energy usage (electricity and heating)
  - Diet type
  - Consumption habits (shopping)
  - Waste generation

- **Visual Analytics**:
  - Doughnut chart showing emission breakdown by category
  - Comparison with US average emissions
  - Detailed breakdown of each emission source

- **Personalized Recommendations**:
  - Action items tailored to your lifestyle
  - Potential carbon savings from each recommendation
  - Tips for sustainable living

### Education Section
- Information about major emission sources
- Real statistics and facts
- Impact of different choices

## 📊 Emission Categories

1. **🚗 Transportation** - Car and flight emissions
2. **⚡ Energy** - Electricity and heating usage
3. **🍽️ Diet** - Food consumption impact
4. **🛍️ Shopping** - Clothing and consumer goods
5. **♻️ Waste** - General waste generation

## 🚀 How to Use

### Local Setup
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Musfira-Shaik/carbon-footprint-awareness.git
   cd carbon-footprint-awareness
   ```

2. Open in browser:
   - Open `index.html` in your web browser
   - Or serve using a simple HTTP server:
   ```bash
   python -m http.server 8000
   # Then visit http://localhost:8000
   ```

### Using the Calculator
1. Fill in your lifestyle information
2. Click "Calculate My Footprint"
3. View your results and get personalized recommendations
4. Implement suggestions to reduce your carbon footprint

## 💡 Carbon Reduction Tips

### Quick Wins
- **Walk or bike** for trips under 1 mile
- **Use public transit** instead of driving
- **Switch to LED bulbs** - saves 75% on lighting energy
- **Try Meatless Mondays** - reduces diet emissions by ~40%
- **Buy secondhand** clothing instead of new

### Long-term Changes
- Install solar panels
- Switch to renewable energy plans
- Improve home insulation
- Buy electric or hybrid vehicles
- Adopt plant-based diet
- Support carbon offset programs

## 📈 Environmental Impact

- **US Average**: 16 metric tons CO2e/year per person
- **Global Average**: ~4 metric tons CO2e/year per person
- **Target (2030)**: 2-3 metric tons CO2e/year per person

### Quick Stats
- One round-trip flight to Europe = ~1 ton CO2e
- Driving a car for a year = ~4.6 tons CO2e
- Vegetarian diet saves = ~0.9 tons CO2e/year vs meat-heavy
- Solar panel installation saves = ~3-5 tons CO2e/year

## 🌱 Trees & Carbon Offset
- **1 mature tree** absorbs ~20-25 kg CO2/year
- To offset 1 metric ton of CO2, plant ~50 trees
- Support reforestation projects to compensate for unavoidable emissions
