What is a Carbon Footprint?

🚗 Transportation

Cars, planes, and public transport emit significant CO2. Average car emits 4.6 metric tons of CO2 per year.

⚡ Energy Usage

Electricity generation is a major source of emissions. Switching to renewable energy can reduce impact by up to 80%.

🍽️ Diet

Meat production has high carbon emissions. A vegetarian diet can reduce carbon footprint by 1.5 tons/year.

♻️ Consumption

Manufacturing and waste contribute significantly. Reducing, reusing, and recycling helps lower your impact.

Simple Steps to Reduce Carbon Footprint

🚴

Walk or Bike

Avoid short car trips. Walking and cycling produce zero emissions.

🚌

Use Public Transit

Public transport reduces per-person emissions by 45% compared to driving.

💡

Energy Efficiency

Use LED bulbs, insulate your home, and use energy-efficient appliances.

🥗

Eat Less Meat

Try Meatless Mondays. Plant-based foods have lower carbon footprints.

📦

Reduce Consumption

Buy less, choose quality over quantity, and buy secondhand when possible.

☀️

Renewable Energy

Switch to renewable energy sources or install solar panels at home.
